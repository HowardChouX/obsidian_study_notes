### **学术英语摘要写作作业1: 造纸业 (扩展版)**

The paper and pulp industry went through very difficult times between 1989 and 2015.（造纸和制浆行业在1989年至2015年间经历了非常困难的时期。）
During this period, companies faced strong competition from around the world and had to change the way they worked completely.（在此期间，公司面临着来自世界各地的激烈竞争，不得不完全改变其工作方式。）
This study wanted to find out what makes some companies successful for a long time, even when the market and technology keep changing.（这项研究旨在找出是什么让一些公司在市场和技术不断变化的情况下能够长期成功。）
The goal was to create a success model that other companies in the industry could learn from and copy.（目标是创建一个成功模型，供行业内的其他公司学习和复制。）
To do this, the researchers looked at a lot of industry data.（为此，研究人员查阅了大量行业数据。）
They tried to find the key factors that help companies succeed and how these factors work together.（他们试图找出帮助公司成功的关键因素以及这些因素如何协同作用。）
The main idea of the study is that there is not just one way to succeed.（该研究的主要观点是，成功并非只有一种方式。）
Different companies can use different combinations of factors to achieve long-term success.（不同的公司可以使用不同的因素组合来实现长期成功。）
The study used a special research method called fsQCA (fuzzy-set Qualitative Comparative Analysis).（该研究使用了一种称为fsQCA（模糊集定性比较分析）的特殊研究方法。）
This method is very good for studying situations where many different causes work together to create a result.（该方法非常适合研究多种不同原因共同作用产生结果的情况。）
The data for the study came from many different sources between 1989 and 2015, including news articles, academic research papers, and reports from the industry itself.（研究数据来自1989年至2015年间的多种不同来源，包括新闻文章、学术研究论文以及行业自身报告。）
The most important job of the fsQCA method is to find out which combinations of conditions are necessary and which are enough for a company to be successful.（fsQCA方法最重要的任务是确定哪些条件组合是公司成功的必要条件，哪些是充分条件。）

### **学术英语摘要写作作业5: 饮食与健康 (扩展版)**

Can eating less help you live longer?（少吃能帮助你活得更久吗？）
For many years, scientific evidence has shown that a diet with very few calories can extend the lifespan of animals like mice and monkeys.（多年来，科学证据表明，低热量饮食可以延长小鼠和猴子等动物的寿命。）
A recent study now suggests that this way of eating might have positive effects on humans too.（最近的一项研究表明，这种饮食方式可能对人类也有积极影响。）
The more scientists learn about calorie restriction, the more it seems like a kind of medical wonder.（科学家们对热量限制了解得越多，它就越像一种医学奇迹。）
A study published this week in the journal *Science* provides strong evidence.（本周发表在《科学》杂志上的一项研究提供了有力证据。）
It followed monkeys that were on a calorie-restricted diet for 20 years.（该研究跟踪了进行热量限制饮食20年的猴子。）
These monkeys not only lived longer than monkeys who ate a normal diet, but they also had brains that seemed younger.（这些猴子不仅比正常饮食的猴子活得更长，而且它们的大脑看起来更年轻。）

A woman might eat only 1500 to 1700 calories, compared to the national average recommendation of 2000 calories.（女性可能只摄入1500至1700卡路里，而全国平均推荐摄入量为2000卡路里。）
It is important to understand that this does not mean the food they eat is of poor quality or not nutritious.（重要的是要理解，这并不意味着他们吃的食物质量差或没有营养。）
The focus is on reducing the total amount of energy (calories) consumed while still getting all the necessary vitamins and nutrients from healthy foods.（重点是在减少总能量（卡路里）摄入的同时，仍然从健康食物中获得所有必需的维生素和营养素。）

The study then examined how this public participation affected a city's ability to reduce both pollution and carbon emissions at the same time.（然后，该研究考察了这种公众参与如何影响城市同时减少污染和碳排放的能力。）

The study looked at *how* this happens.（该研究考察了这是*如何*发生的。）
It found two main ways.（它发现了两种主要方式。）
First, public participation strengthens government environmental rules.（首先，公众参与加强了政府的环境法规。）

### **学术英语摘要写作作业9: 水的沸腾实验 (扩展版)**

Through a simple experiment, we can observe how water changes at different temperatures and learn the science behind it.（通过一个简单的实验，我们可以观察水在不同温度下的变化，并了解其背后的科学原理。）
This article will introduce the basic steps and observations of a water boiling experiment.（本文将介绍水沸腾实验的基本步骤和观察结果。）

First, pour an appropriate amount of water into the beaker.（首先，向烧杯中倒入适量的水。）

Next, heat the water in the beaker with the alcohol lamp.（接下来，用酒精灯加热烧杯中的水。）

### **学术英语摘要写作作业10: 云计算的基本原理及其应用 (扩展版)**

Cloud computing is a technology that provides computing resources like servers, storage, and databases through the internet.（云计算是一种通过互联网提供服务器、存储和数据库等计算资源的技术。）
It allows users to use these resources whenever they need them, without having to buy and maintain physical hardware.（它允许用户在需要时使用这些资源，而无需购买和维护物理硬件。）
This article will explain the basic principles of cloud computing and its applications in various fields.（本文将解释云计算的基本原理及其在各个领域的应用。）
The definition of cloud computing is a computing model based on the internet, where users can access and use computing resources managed by cloud service providers through virtualization technology.（云计算的定义是一种基于互联网的计算模型，用户可以通过虚拟化技术访问和使用云服务提供商管理的计算资源。）
There are three main types of cloud computing: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud.（云计算主要有三种类型：公有云、私有云和混合云。）
Public Cloud is managed by third-party providers and is open to the public.（公有云由第三方提供商管理，对公众开放。）
Private Cloud is managed by a company for its own use.（私有云由公司自行管理供自身使用。）
Hybrid Cloud combines the features of both.（混合云结合了两者的特点。）
Cloud computing is widely used in many areas, including enterprise resource management, big data analysis, and the Internet of Things.（云计算广泛应用于许多领域，包括企业资源管理、大数据分析和物联网。）
It provides companies with flexible and efficient computing resources, reducing their operating costs.（它为公司提供灵活高效的计算资源，降低其运营成本。）
For example, a company can use cloud services to store data, run applications, and analyze large amounts of information without investing in expensive servers.（例如，公司可以使用云服务来存储数据、运行应用程序和分析大量信息，而无需投资昂贵的服务器。）
